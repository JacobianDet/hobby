About Me

I am Aadarsh Singh, a prefinal year student of the deemed Indian Institute of Technology, Dhanbad pursuing Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering. Apart from academics, I love to collect coins and edit videos & images. These small website is a collection of end products of some of my hobbies.

In my coin collection you can view a variety of old indian coins ranging from the quarter anna of the British era to the newest 50 paise coin. Although these are not used these days but they definitely contribute much to the history of India. Also my collection contains some rare coins like the 5 rupee coin minted on the 50th Anniversary of United Nations.

Apart from collecting coins, I also love to edit videos and photos for fun. Normally, I use Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere Pro. One of the videos also involves use of Videoscribe, a software to create presentation videos. My image editing works involves framing quotes into images to make them appealing. All of which are published with the watermark "Experience Unleashed".

In my leisure time, I also love to pursue small courses to improve my soft skills like courses on time management, efficient working, etc.Not only this, I also enjoy exploring new dimensions of the big world of Computer Science. The certificates of my hard earned achievements are also attached on this website.
